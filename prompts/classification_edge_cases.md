# Classification Edge Cases — Decision Log

Real examples from the ACQ Vantage community where classification isn't obvious.
Use these as precedent when similar posts appear.

## Pattern: AI tool used for a specific domain task
- If the post is about HOW to use an AI tool, it's ai_tools
- If AI is mentioned incidentally while discussing another topic, classify by the primary topic

## Pattern: Ad spend question that's really about metrics
- If the core decision is "should I spend more/less on ads," it's paid_ads even if they're asking about numbers
- tracking_analytics is about the measurement infrastructure itself (tools, attribution models, dashboards)

## Pattern: GHL / automation platform problems
- Platform/tool configuration issues → ai_tools
- Strategy questions about what to send or who to target → email_outreach or lead_gen_funnels

## Pattern: Show-up rate / delivery problems
- Converting leads already in funnel (show rates, booking rates) → lead_gen_funnels
- Reaching new people (cold email, cold DM, list building) → email_outreach

## Pattern: Offer structure vs lead gen
- Offer creation, pricing, trial structure, retention mechanics → sales_offers
- Landing pages, VSLs, opt-in flows, conversion optimization → lead_gen_funnels

## Pattern: Limiting beliefs / mindset posts
- Business context + mindset as growth blocker → scaling_strategy
- Generic "believe in yourself" with no business context → conversational

## Pattern: Event logistics / resource sharing
- Event links, recording shares, schedule changes, access requests → conversational + neutral

## Pattern: Hiring a specific role
- "Who should I hire / where do I find this person" → hiring, regardless of what the role does

## Pattern: Quote/tweet shares
- Quote share with no original insight → conversational
- Quote share with extracted lessons and business framing → classify by the topic of the lesson
